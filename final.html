Kids

When you think of Miami Beach, you might be thinking of a small bikinis and gorgeous models. You also can imagine the saucy and colorful nightlife along Ocean Drive and Lincoln road. But, Miami Beach is so much more. As a parent, taking children on a Miami Beach vacation will unlock an entirely new side of the famous destination. From parks to interact with nature, to beaches that thrill all ages - there are activities for every personality type. Combined with plenty of roomy vacation homes to choose from, Miami Beach is a vacation mecca for families with children.

Beaches

Miami offer multiple beaches with clean blue water and warm sand. If you do not like places with a lot of crowd, please come to Hollywood beach. if you do like loud parties till late - South Beach is waiting for you.
